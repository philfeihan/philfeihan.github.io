ABOUT ME

I've never been a one-trick pony. I built a decade-long career in design, leading products from concept to launch. But I wanted to do more than design—I wanted to build, to understand the logic behind the interface, and to speak the language of technology fluently.

So I pivoted again, back to school. I'm now a Data Science student at Dalhousie University, merging a designer's mind with a developer's skillset (front-end, Python and more). My goal is to create digital products that are not only intuitive and beautiful but also deeply intelligent and data-informed.

I'm inherently curious and see technology as a universal passport to new worlds.

Oh, and I think and create in user flows, code, and visual design.
